Heinz

Heinz is a lightweight framework for storytellers that want to focus on what really matters - the content.

Heinz comes with a beautiful design out of the box and helps you build readable content. Lots of white-space and a clean look make every story shine.

Heinz was created by 6 Media Engineering Students with lots of love in beautiful Switzerland.

Built with HTML, Sass & jQuery.
